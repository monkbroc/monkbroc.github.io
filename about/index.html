About me

My background is in computer engineering. I graduated from McGill University in Montreal in 2005.

I worked for 9 years in the Detroit, MI area for Bosch, a large automotive supplier, doing embedded engine controls in C.

At that point, I decided to reboot my career and to found a startup with a long time friend of mine. Alan Mond and I started MuniRent in 2014.

When I'm not busy exploring the latest fad in web and application development, I like to spend time playing with my two kids and going skiing.
